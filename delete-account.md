# Delete your Androverse account                          
                                                                                                                          
  You can delete your Androverse account and all associated data in two ways:                                             
                                          
  ## Option 1 — In-app (recommended)                                                                                      
                                                                                                                          
  1. Open Androverse
  2. Go to the **Profile** tab                                                                                            
  3. Tap **Delete account**                                 
  4. Confirm                                                                                                              
                                                            
  This immediately removes your authentication data, learning progress, challenge history, and leaderboard entry.
                                                                                                                          
  ## Option 2 — By email                      
                                                                                                                          
  If you can't access the app, email **jose.ignacio97@gmail.com** from the address associated with your account. Include
  the subject line **"Delete Androverse account"**. We'll process the request within 30 days.                             
                                                            
  ## What gets deleted                                                                                                    
                                                            
  - Your account (email, display name, Firebase UID)                                                                      
  - All learning progress (completed lessons, topics)       
  - All challenge history (answers, XP, streaks)
  - Your leaderboard entry                                                                                                
  - Any cached data on your device        
                                                                                                                          
  ## What may remain                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                          
  - Anonymous, aggregated usage data with no link to your identity                                                        
  - Backups may retain data for up to 30 additional days before being overwritten
                                              
  For privacy questions, contact jose.ignacio97@gmail.com
