Privacy Policy for Androverse                                                                                                                                                      
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  Last updated: April 20, 2026                                                                                                                                                       
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  Androverse ("we", "our", or "the app") is an educational app for Android developers. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.                    
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  1. Data we collect                                                                                                                                                                 
                                                            
  When you use Androverse, we collect:                                                                                                                                               
   
  - Account information: email address and display name, via Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In or email/password).                                                             
  - Learning progress: topics started, lessons completed, last accessed date.
  - Challenge activity: challenges answered, correct/incorrect results, time spent, XP earned, level, streak, accuracy, remaining lives.                                             
  - Leaderboard data: your display name, initials, and total XP — shown to other authenticated users on the global leaderboard.                                                      
  - Push notification token: to send you streak reminders (only if you grant notification permission).                                                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  We do not collect: location, contacts, photos, audio, device identifiers beyond what Firebase requires, or payment information.                                                    
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  2. How we use your data                                                                                                                                                            
                                                            
  - Authentication: to sign you in and keep your session secure.                                                                                                                     
  - App functionality: to track your learning progress, XP, and streaks across devices.
  - Leaderboard: to rank users globally based on XP.                                                                                                                                 
  - Notifications: to remind you to maintain your daily streak (optional).                                                                                                           
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  We do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or share it with third parties beyond the service providers listed below.                                                        
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  3. Third-party services                                                                                                                                                            
                                                            
  Androverse uses the following Google services to operate:                                                                                                                          
                                                            
  - Firebase Authentication — manages user accounts.                                                                                                                                 
  - Cloud Firestore — stores your progress, challenge history, and leaderboard entry.
  - Firebase Cloud Messaging — delivers push notifications.                                                                                                                          
                                                            
  These services are provided by Google LLC and are subject to https://policies.google.com/privacy.                                                                                  
                                                            
  4. Data storage and security                                                                                                                                                       
                                                            
  - Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers (encrypted in transit via HTTPS).                                                                                                 
  - Progress data is also cached locally on your device for offline access.
  - Access to your account data is restricted to you via Firestore security rules.                                                                                                   
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  5. Your rights                                                                                                                                                                     
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  You can, at any time:                                                                                                                                                              
   
  - Sign out from the Profile section.                                                                                                                                               
  - Delete your account from Profile → Delete account. This permanently removes your authentication data, learning progress, challenge history, and leaderboard entry. This action
  cannot be undone.                                                                                                                                                                  
  - Revoke notification permission from your device's system settings.
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  6. Children                                               
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  Androverse is intended for developers aged 16 or older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact 
  us to have it removed.
                                                                                                                                                                                     
  7. Data retention                                         

  We retain your data as long as your account is active. Once you delete your account, all your personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.                
   
  8. Changes to this policy                                                                                                                                                          
                                                            
  We may update this policy occasionally. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the 
  updated policy.
